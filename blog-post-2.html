My Go-To Toolkit for University Tasks

Posted on June 18, 2025

One of the biggest challenges at university isn't just learning the material, but managing the process of learning. For a recent assignment, I had to think about the specific digital tools I'd use for different academic tasks. It was a great exercise in being intentional about my workflow instead of just using what's familiar.

For Organization:

Google Calendar is my source of truth for deadlines and class schedules. For managing the actual work, I use Todoist to break down large assignments into smaller, actionable steps. It's incredibly satisfying to check things off the list.

For Note-Taking and Research:

I live in Notion. It's where I take lecture notes, embed slides, and build a personal wiki for each course. When it comes to serious research, Zotero is non-negotiable. It saves all my sources and generates bibliographies automatically, which has saved me countless hours.

About Me

Hello! I'm Michael Kuo, a passionate and curious student pursuing a degree in Cognitive Science at Georgia State University. My academic journey began in Atlanta, GA, which has given me a unique, cross-cultural perspective on how we think, learn, and interact with the world.

My primary interest lies at the nexus of technology and the human mind. I'm fascinated by how digital tools shape our cognition, how artificial intelligence can model human thought, and how we can design better technology by understanding the principles of psychology and neuroscience. This course, "Computer Skills for the Information Age," has been instrumental in providing me with the foundational skills to explore these interests in a practical way.

Through my coursework, I've developed skills in academic research, information literacy, web design, and critical evaluation of digital resources. This portfolio is a living document of that journey. When I'm not studying, I enjoy hiking, learning new languages, and exploring the vibrant food!
